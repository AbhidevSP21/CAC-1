The Weeknd's Works

Mixtapes

The Weeknd gained prominence with mixtapes like "House of Balloons," "Thursday," and "Echoes of Silence."

Albums

The Weeknd's discography includes albums such as "Trilogy," "Kiss Land," "Beauty Behind the Madness," and "After Hours."

Singles

He has released hit singles like "Can't Feel My Face," "Starboy," and "Blinding Lights."

Collaborations

The Weeknd has collaborated with various artists, including Ariana Grande and Drake.
